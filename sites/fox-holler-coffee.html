Our Story

Rooted in Effingham

Fox Holler Coffee was built on a simple idea: great coffee should be accessible. Owner Seth Wilson started with a passion for craft roasting and turned it into a community gathering spot. Today, we roast every batch in-house, serve a scratch kitchen menu, and make it easy to grab your favorite drink on the go.
