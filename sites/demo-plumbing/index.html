What We Do

Our Services

From a dripping faucet to a full HVAC system replacement, Hargrove handles it all — promptly and professionally.

Plumbing Repair

Leaks, burst pipes, fixture replacement, and everything in between. No job too small — we treat every call like it's the only one on the schedule.

Water Heaters

Installation and repair of tank and tankless water heaters. Same-day service available. We carry common units on the truck so you're not waiting days without hot water.

HVAC

Heating and cooling installation, maintenance, and emergency repair for all major brands. Keeping Monticello comfortable through central Illinois winters and summers alike.

Drain Cleaning

Clogged drains cleared fast with professional hydro-jetting and snake equipment. Kitchen, bath, floor drains, and sewer lines — we've seen it all and cleared it all.

New Construction

Rough-in plumbing for new builds and major renovations. We work seamlessly with your contractor or ours — on schedule and to code, every time.

Water Treatment

Water softeners, filtration systems, and well pump service for rural properties throughout Piatt County. Better water starts with the right equipment, properly installed.
